Triple Pass Drum Dryer

Triple pass rotary drum dryer, also known as three drum dryer, differs in structure from a single pass drum dryer. It consists of three drums of varying diameters nested together, forming three drying chambers: an inner layer, a middle layer, and an outer layer. Unlike single pass drum dryers, triple pass dryers do not require to be set on a sloped base or rely on gravity to move materials. Instead, they rely on pneumatic conveying within the drums to loosen materials to specified positions.

Pressure Vessel

A pressure vessel is a container designed to hold gases or liquids at a pressure substantially different from the ambient pressure. These vessels are essential components in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, power generation, and manufacturing.

Storage Vessels

Used to store gases or liquids at high pressures. Examples include gas cylinders, compressed air tanks, and propane tanks.

Heat Exchangers

Transfer heat between two or more fluids under pressure. Common in industries like chemical processing, power plants, and refrigeration.

Process Vessels

Hold and sometimes react chemicals under pressure. Examples include distillation columns, reactors, and separators.

Boilers

Generate steam by heating water under pressure. Widely used in power generation and heating systems.

Industrial chimneys

Industrial chimneys, also known as flue gas stacks, are tall structures designed to vent smoke, gases, and combustion products from boilers, furnaces, or other industrial systems into the atmosphere. They play a critical role in maintaining air quality by safely dispersing harmful gases at higher altitudes, reducing their impact on the surrounding environment.

Boiling House

A boiling house is a key facility in sugar production, particularly in traditional cane sugar mills. Its primary function is to process sugarcane juice into raw or refined sugar through a series of heating and evaporation steps. This facility is crucial for concentrating sugarcane juice, crystallizing sugar, and separating it from the molasses.

Juice Concentration

The sugarcane juice extracted from the milling section is first clarified and then concentrated in evaporators to reduce water content.

Crystallization

Concentrated juice is further boiled under controlled conditions to form sugar crystals.

Separation

Crystallized sugar is separated from molasses using centrifuges.

Molasses Recovery

Residual molasses, which is the by-product of sugar crystallization, is collected for further processing or sale.

EOT Crane

An EOT Crane (Electric Overhead Traveling Crane) is a type of overhead crane commonly used in industrial environments for lifting and moving heavy materials. These cranes are widely employed in manufacturing, warehouses, workshops, and maintenance facilities due to their versatility and efficiency in material handling.

Types of EOT Cranes 1) Single Girder EOT Crane
- Design: Has a single bridge girder supported by end trucks. A hoist is mounted on the bottom flange of the single girder.
- Capacity: Generally used for lighter loads (up to 20 tons).
- Span: Suitable for smaller spans (up to 20 meters).

2) Double Girder EOT Crane:
- Design: Features two parallel bridge girders with the hoist traveling on top of them, providing greater stability.
- Capacity: Handles heavier loads (up to several hundred tons).
Span: Suitable for larger spans (up to 35 meters or more).
- Height of Lift: Provides greater hook height, which means it can lift loads higher than a single girder crane.
